Discover about us

Leading Hotel in the city

This hotel is located in the centre of Baku, just a 3-minute walk from Nizami Metro Station. Free Wi-Fi and a 24-hour front desk are featured at Caspian Palace Hotel. The elegant, air-conditioned rooms offer warm-coloured interiors and classic-style decor with deep red furnishings. Every room includes a flat-screen TV, a minibar and a private bathroom. Guests can have breakfast in their room. A restaurant serving European and Azerbaijani cuisine is located in the same building with the hotel. A variety of cafes and shops can be found within a 5-minute walk from the hotel.
